**OSOlogic PRO** is an open-source industrial PLC platform designed for maximum flexibility and adaptability. It allows you to read sensors, control outputs, and manage complex automation workflows. Its modular architecture and standard interfaces make it a perfect fit for a wide range of industrial and home automation systems, allowing developers and integrators to modify and adapt the core engine to their specific needs.

This manual explains how to operate the system — **no programming knowledge required**, though its open nature allows for deep customization.

## How to Interact with the PLC

There are **6 ways** to communicate with OSOlogic PRO. Choose the one that best fits your workflow:

## Network Access Summary

All services are accessible over the network. Here are the default addresses:

| Service | URL / Address | Port |
|---------|--------------|------|
| **Service Manager** | `https://<PLC_IP>:8080` | 8080 |
| **Database GUI** | `https://<PLC_IP>:8082` | 8082 |
| **phpMyAdmin** | `http://<PLC_IP>/phpmyadmin` | 80 |
| **Modbus TCP** | `<PLC_IP>` | 502 |
| **MQTT Broker** | `<PLC_IP>` | 1883 |
| **Node-RED** | `https://<PLC_IP>:1880` | 1880 |
| **MariaDB** | `<PLC_IP>` | 3306 |

<Note>
  Replace `<PLC_IP>` with the actual IP address of your PLC. You can find it in your network configuration or by asking your system administrator.
</Note>
